Something About her

Echo Bloom is a musician, artist, and storyteller from San Francisco, California. She graduated from the Academy of Art University in San Francisco and has worked as a concept artist in animation and game for seven years. She is a multifaceted artist who loves to explore a wide range of genres, from rock to folk, and has a strong passion for storytelling and creating music that speaks to the heart. Her music is often deeply personal and intimate, and her lyrics often touch on themes of love, life, and faith. With her unique sound and passionate delivery, Echo Bloom has quickly become an artist to watch. She is an artist who will continue to push boundaries and create beautiful, meaningful music.
